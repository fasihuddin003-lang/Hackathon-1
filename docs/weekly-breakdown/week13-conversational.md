# Week 13: Conversational Robotics

## Topics Covered

- Integrating GPT models for conversational AI in robots
- Speech recognition and natural language understanding
- Multi-modal interaction: speech, gesture, vision

## Learning Objectives

By the end of Week 13, students should be able to:
- Integrate GPT models with ROS 2 for conversation
- Implement speech-to-text and text-to-speech pipelines
- Design multi-modal interaction systems
- Complete the capstone autonomous humanoid project
