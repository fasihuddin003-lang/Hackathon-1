# Weeks 1-2: Introduction to Physical AI

## Topics Covered

- Foundations of Physical AI and embodied intelligence
- From digital AI to robots that understand physical laws
- Overview of humanoid robotics landscape
- Sensor systems: LIDAR, cameras, IMUs, force/torque sensors

## Learning Objectives

By the end of Week 2, students should be able to:
- Define Physical AI and explain its importance
- Contrast digital AI with embodied AI
- Identify the key components of a humanoid robot
- Describe how sensors enable robots to perceive their environment
